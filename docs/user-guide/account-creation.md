# Account Creation & Sign In

## How to Create an Account

### Steps
1. Open or [**download Liberdus**](https://liberdus.com/site/download/).  
2. On the home screen, tap **Create Account**.  
3. Input your username.  
   - The password must contain a **minimum of 3 characters** and a **maximum of 15**. The characters that are allowed are Letters, numbers, emojis, and symbols. No spaces are allowed.
4. Wait a few seconds while your account keys are generated.  
5. Once complete, your new account will appear on the **dashboard**.

### Important
- Each Liberdus account is **unique** and **cryptographically secured** using **quantum cryptography**, future-proofing messages.  
- You can create multiple accounts later from the main menu if needed.

## How to Sign In to an Existing Account

Use the Sign In option to access an account that’s already been created or restored on your device.

### When the Sign In Button Appears
- The **Sign In** button will only be available if **at least one account** exists on your device.  
- If you have not created or restored any accounts yet, the button will be **disabled**.

### Steps: Signing In (Single Account)
If there is only one account stored on your device:

1. Open **Liberdus** and tap **Sign In**.  
2. Liberdus will automatically sign you into your only account- no selection required.  
3. You will be taken directly into your **account dashboard**.

### Steps: Signing In (Multiple Accounts)
If there are two or more accounts stored on your device:

1. Open **Liberdus** and tap **Sign In**.  
2. A dropdown list titled **Select Account** will appear.  
3. Choose the account you want to access from the dropdown menu.  
4. Tap the **Sign In** button to confirm.  
5. You will be signed into the selected account and taken to your **dashboard**.

### Important
- Liberdus supports **multiple accounts** on the same device, and each account is fully **independent**.  
- To remove old accounts you no longer use, go to **Menu → Remove**.  
- You must sign in each time you want to **switch between accounts**.

## How to Sign Out

Securely log out of your Liberdus account on your current device.

### Steps: Signing Out
1. Tap the **⚙️ Settings** icon in the top-right corner of the main screen.  
2. Scroll to the bottom of the **Settings** menu.  
3. Tap **Sign Out** (displayed in red).  
4. Confirm if prompted.  
5. You’ll be returned to the **main Liberdus login screen**.

### Important
If you plan to use Liberdus on another device, export your backup first from **Settings → Backup → Download Backup File**, then import it on the new device during **Sign In**.
