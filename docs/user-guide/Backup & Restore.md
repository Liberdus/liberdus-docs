## ğŸ’¾ How to Back Up an Account

**Purpose:**  
Secure your data by saving an offline backup file.

### Steps
1. Open the **Menu**.  
2. Tap **Backup**.  
3. Click the **Download** button.  
4. Choose a destination folder on your device to save the backup.  
5. Ensure **Save File** is clicked, then click **Okay**.

### Notes
- The backup file contains your **account keys**, **settings**, and other important information.  
- Store it safely (e.g., **encrypted USB drive**, **password manager**, or a secure offline record).  
- We recommend **encrypting your backups** to prevent data exposure to third parties.
