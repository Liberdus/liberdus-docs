# Wallet Balance & History

## How to View Your LIB Wallet Balance

Check how much **LIB** you currently own, along with its estimated value in **USD**.

### Steps: Viewing Your Balance

1. Tap the **Wallet** tab at the bottom of the screen.  
2. At the top, you will see your **Total Balance**, shown in **USD**.  
3. Under **Assets**, find **Liberdus (LIB)**.  
4. Your **LIB amount** is displayed on the right, while the **USD equivalent** appears underneath it.  

You can instantly see your LIB holdings and their approximate USD market value.

### Important

| Detail | Explanation |
|---------|--------------|
| **Shows LIB + USD** | LIB is your actual balance; USD is a visual conversion for clarity. |
| **Live conversion** | The USD figure is based on the current market rate and may fluctuate. |
| **Future assets** | The Wallet tab may support additional assets later, each listed under ‚ÄúAssets.‚Äù |
| **Total Balance** | If multiple assets are supported in the future, Total Balance will show the combined USD value of all assets. |

## How to View Your Wallet History & Understand Transaction Types

Review all past wallet transactions including **Sent**, **Received**, and **Deleted** records- and quickly jump back to the related chat when needed.

### Steps: Viewing Transaction History

1. Tap the **Wallet** tab.  
2. Tap **History**.  
3. If needed, choose the asset (e.g., **Liberdus (LIB)**) from the dropdown.  
4. Browse the list to see all **Sent**, **Received**, and **Deleted** transactions.

### Important

- **Memos** help you remember the purpose of each transaction.
